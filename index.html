Recipe #1 Chocolate Chip Cookies

  1. Recipe #1
  • Time: 8 to 10 minutes
  • Serves: no clue
Ingredients Quantity
flour, baking soda, salt, butter, sugar, vanilla, eggs and chocolate chips. not the slightest clue

Step 1: Heat oven to 375°F. In a small bowl, mix flour, baking soda, and salt then set it aside.
Step 2: In a large bowl, beat soft butter and sugers with an electric mixer on mediam speed, or mix with spoon for about one minute or until fluffy.
Step 3: Beat in egg and vanilla until smooth. Stir in flour mixture just until blended and then stir in in the chocolate chips.
Step 4: Onto ungreased cookie sheets, drop dough by rounded tablespoonfuls 2 inches apart.
Step 5: Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until light brown (centers will be soft). Cool 2 minute; remove from cookie sheet to cooling rack. Cool completely, about 30 minutes. Store covered in airtight container.

Recipe #2 Cake Pops

  1. Recipe #2
  • Time: 15 min?
  • Serves: 1 to 2?
Ingredients Quantity
vanilla cake, frosting, cake pop stick, nonpareils, and styrofoam. not in the slightest clue

Step 1: Bake the vanilla cake, let it cool and remove the outer brownish layer with a knife.
Step 2:Crumble the cake into small pieces and mix it with the frosting.
Step 3: Form the crumbs into cake balls. Dip the end of a cake pop stick into melted candy melts and insert the stick halfway through each cake ball.
Step 4: Freeze the vanilla cake pops for 15 minutes.
Step 5: Pour melted candy melts into a tall narrow cup and dip each cake pop into the candy melts. Then tap off excess candy and sprinkle with nonpareils.
Step 6: Place the finished vanilla cake pops into a block of styrofoam so that it can cool and stiffen.

Recipe #4 Chocolate Pecan Fondue

  1. Recipe #4
  • Time: 15 min
  • Serves:as many as wanted
Ingredients Quantity
half-and-half cream, honey, semisweet chocolate (broken into small pieces),finely chopped pecans, vanilla extract, Fresh fruit and shortbread cookies 1 - 1/3 cups

Step 1:In a heavy saucepan over low heat, combine cream and honey; heat until warm. Add chocolate; stir until melted. Stir in pecans and vanilla.
Step 2:Transfer to a fondue pot or a 1-1/2-qt. slow cooker and keep warm. Serve with fruit and cookies.

Recipe #5 Chai Cupcakes

  1. Recipe #5
  • Time: 25 min
  • Serves:12 Human Beings
Ingredients Quantity
1/2 teaspoon each ground ginger, cinnamon, cardamom and cloves, pepper, butter, (softened), sugar, large egg, room temperature, vanilla extract, cake flour, baking powder, salt, 2% milk, frosting: butters(softened), confectioners' sugar, vanilla extract, 2% milk 1 dozen

Step 1:In a small bowl, combine the ginger, cinnamon, cardamom, cloves and pepper; set aside.
Step 2:In a large bowl, cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy, 5-7 minutes. Beat in egg and vanilla. Combine the flour, baking powder, salt and 1-1/2 teaspoons spice mixture. Gradually add to creamed mixture alternately with milk, beating well after each addition.
Step 3:Fill 12 paper-lined muffin cups two-thirds full. Bake at 350° until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean, 24-28 minutes. Cool for 10 minutes before removing from pans to wire racks to cool completely.
Step 4:In a large bowl, beat butter until fluffy; beat in the confectioners' sugar, vanilla and remaining spice mixture until smooth. Add enough milk to reach desired consistency. Pipe frosting over cupcakes.
